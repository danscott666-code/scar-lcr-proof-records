Personally, I am very disorganised, so planning is a problem for me. I believe that is due to me having a degree of ADHD. So I have always been a big fan of cramming and what I would do myself is take one subject at a time with say, a week leading up to the actual exam and totally focus on an individual subject. Due to my short term memory being very strong and my ability to hyper focus for short periods this technique really works for me. So leading up to the exam, I would gather any past papers, get information from teachers on what is most likely to be included in the exam, go through all my work from lessons in my books and I would focus on that first, and I would do the bulk of my revising for each subject in a really concentrated and intense way and do it literally up to the moment before the exam. So my technique is to write everything out and read it back to myself, and then maybe have questions and get someone to ask me questions and try to answer those questions. But predominantly it is writing everything out to try and get it branded into my brain.
