SCAR_AI_AUTH_DS01 — README
Version: 1.3 — PILOT COMPLETE / FULL COLLECTION IN PROGRESS

PURPOSE
-------
SCAR_AI_AUTH_DS01 is a controlled, audit-grade dataset for validating SCAR/LCR
transfer from scriptural corpora to a human-vs-AI expository text setting.

SCOPE
-----
Full dataset (design scope):
- Prompts: P01-P10
- Samples: 40 (20 human, 20 AI)

Pilot execution scope:
- Prompts: P01-P05 only
- Samples: 20 (10 human, 10 AI)

The pilot operates on a strict subset of the full design. No modification of the
design files is permitted during pilot execution.

METHODOLOGY LIMITATIONS (FORMALLY DECLARED)
--------------------------------------------
The following limitations are declared before GitHub publication per main
thread ruling 2026-04-14. They do not invalidate the pilot result but must
be stated explicitly.

1. INDEPENDENCE CAVEAT
   Samples are not fully independent. Two instances per prompt per condition
   share task context. The permutation test assumes independence. The p=0.0139
   result should be interpreted with this caveat.

2. MODEL SPECIFICITY
   All 10 AI samples were generated by GPT-5.3 Instant via ChatGPT app
   (interface-reported version, not API-pinned). The finding is currently
   model-specific. DS02 must include at least one additional AI system before
   any model-agnostic generalisation claim can be supported. DS01 does not
   claim model-agnostic results.

3. SINGLE-AUTHOR HUMAN SAMPLES
   All 10 pilot human samples were written by the dataset creator (Danny Scott).
   This is a known methodological limitation. A hostile reviewer will raise it.
   DS02 should recruit at least two independent human writers blind to the
   research hypothesis. For DS01, this limitation is declared and not obscured.

4. INDIVIDUAL THRESHOLD NOT YET DERIVED
   The pilot result establishes group-level separation. Individual classification
   thresholds cannot be derived legitimately from n=10 per group. Completing the
   full 40 samples (n=20 per group) is required before threshold derivation.

KNOWN SIMPLIFICATION
--------------------
Sentence segmentation for Dataset 01 is intentionally simplified and is treated
as a frozen preprocessing approximation rather than a linguistic parser.

RESEARCH ROADMAP NOTES
----------------------
The following are noted for the research roadmap but are outside DS01 scope:

- Hallucination detection application: low-theta outputs (close to model null
  baseline) are candidate hallucination-prone outputs. This is a downstream
  hypothesis requiring its own pre-registered test. Not a DS01 claim.

- Methods paper: the sealed pre-registration protocol, boundary lock
  architecture, hostile audit design, and model-agnostic null framework are
  independently novel contributions. A methods paper describing the framework
  is the recommended first publication output, prioritised over any
  classification accuracy paper.

- DS02 design requirements: second AI model, independent human writers blind
  to research hypothesis, larger sample size.
