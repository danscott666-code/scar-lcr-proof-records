## What This Dataset Is

SCAR_AI_AUTH_DS01 is a 40-sample controlled authorship discrimination dataset produced under the LCR (Law of Coherent Revelation) framework and validated under the SCAR (Structural Coherence Audit and Repair) compliance validator.

It tests whether LCR coherence scoring (theta_ds01) produces a statistically significant group-level separation between human-authored and AI-generated text responses to the same fixed prompts.

Human text scored approximately twice as high on the LCR coherence metric as AI-generated text. The separation is statistically significant at p < 0.001 under a two-sided permutation test on the difference in group means.

## Four Declared Limitations

**1. Independence caveat.** Two samples per prompt share the same task context. The effective statistical independence of the 20 values per class is lower than 20 fully independent samples. This is a known pilot-stage characteristic.

**2. Model specificity.** AI samples were generated by GPT-5.3 Instant (ChatGPT mobile interface, April 2026). Results cannot be generalised to other model families without further testing.

**3. Single-author human samples.** All 20 human samples were written by the dataset creator (Danny Scott) via voice dictation. Single-author data is a declared methodological limitation. DS02 addresses this with a minimum of 5 independent blind human authors.

**4. Individual classification threshold not yet derived.** DS01 establishes group-level separation only. A classification boundary for individual sample decisions has not been derived. Threshold derivation is pre-registered as a DS02 task and will be declared before DS02 collection begins.

---

## Non-Goals

This dataset does not prove universal AI detection. It does not identify model family from output alone. It does not establish legal admissibility in all jurisdictions. It is a controlled pilot demonstrating group-level coherence separation under a pre-registered measurement protocol.

## Reproducibility

The pipeline is fully reproducible from the frozen spec and sample files. RNG seeds are deterministic: `seed = 1337 + row_index`, where row order is fixed in `SCAR_AI_AUTH_DS01_sample_index_COMPLETE.json`. The class comparison RNG seed is 7331. No seed optimisation was performed — seeds were chosen before collection and recorded in the spec.
