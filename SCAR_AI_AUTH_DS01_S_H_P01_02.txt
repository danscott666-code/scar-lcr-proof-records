So these are my instructions on how to make a cup of tea. Firstly, I take a cup from the cupboard. I use my quooker tap to put boiling water in the cup and I swirl it around to warm the cup, then I tip the water out. I put 3 tea bags into the cup because I use a 600 milliliter cup and I like strong tea. Then I again use my quooker tap to fill the cup until it's about 80% full, because it provides boiling water instantly, I don't use a kettle. Then I use organic maple syrup, and I put 2 teaspoons of organic maple syrup into the water with the tea bags, then I mix with a tea spoon. I then use a particular milk which is organic oat and almond, nut milk. Because I like the way tastes and how it reacts with the hot water. It doesn't curdle and go bad like some, non dairy milks do. I then stir and I believe that makes the perfect cup of tea. I do not remove the tea bags. I leave them in the cup the whole time I'm drinking the tea.
