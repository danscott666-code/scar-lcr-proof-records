To begin fill the kettle with fresh cold water and switch on the kettle. I personally use a quooker tap but not everyone has that available. Take a cup from the cupboard and warm it with hot water disregard this water before adding atleast 1 tea bag in the now empty cup. English breakfast tea or Asaam are what I usually prefer and always organic with no plastic in the bag and hot bleached too. 
If you prefer sweeter tea add sugar, maple syrup, or sweetener to taste. 
Once the water in the kettle is boiled, add the boiling water into the cup that has the teabag/s in and stir the water and tea bag around several times with a tea spoon, until the tea bag starts to infuse into the water. If you like add a milk of your choice to the tea infused water, I use organic oat milk. Depending on the strength of the tea you require you should vary the amount of milk.  Remove the tea bag/s or leave it in if you prefer and drink when it's your desired temperature.
